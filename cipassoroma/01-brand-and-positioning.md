# 01 — Brand & Positioning

## Positioning in one sentence

A refined, internationally-minded contemporary-cuisine restaurant in the heart of Rome — near the Pantheon — where romanità and contemporary culinary craft meet, with seasonal ingredients, fresh black truffle, and a curated Italian wine list at the core of the experience.

## Brand pillars (recurring across pages)

1. **Authenticity in central Rome** — "L'autenticità nel cuore di Roma"; geographic pride.
2. **Contemporary cuisine, seasonal ingredients** — Chef Adriano de Angelis; fresh black truffle is a signature.
3. **Wine as a co-protagonist** — broad selection, by-the-glass and by-the-bottle; sommelier Oleg Grossu curates.
4. **Sincere, multilingual hospitality** — international atmosphere; "make every guest feel like the most important one at home".
5. **Earned reputation** — Michelin, TripAdvisor "Best of the Best", Google 4.8.

## Tone of voice

- Italian, warm, slightly literary; uses sensory and emotional words: *"sogno"*, *"passione"*, *"amore"*, *"scintilla"*, *"viaggio"*.
- Confident but humble — explicitly mentions that **bad reviews are taken as growth opportunities**.
- Founders' story is told with vulnerability ("nata in una famiglia povera", "partito come aiuto barista") to reinforce authenticity.

## Signature emotional words

> Magico • Raffinato • Elegante • Gustoso • Love
> (used as a rotating tagline block on the homepage)

## Visual / atmosphere descriptors (their own words)

- *"interni eleganti e musica jazz di sottofondo"*
- *"palazzo storico degli anni '30"*
- *"elegante sala interna e suggestivo dehor"*
- *"ambiente vibrante dal design moderno ed accogliente"*

## What they explicitly promise

- *Cucina e Vino*: sincere Roman contemporary dishes + best Italian-territory wines.
- *Atmosfera*: vibrant modern design, languages and cultures mixing.
- *Garanzia*: honest values; if a guest is not satisfied, they fix it promptly.
- *Riconoscimenti*: Michelin since 2019, 5★ TripAdvisor, 4.8 Google.

## Implicit audience

- Tourists visiting central Rome (Pantheon area) — hence multilingual messaging and an English version.
- Locals seeking a polished, contemporary dinner (note: dinner-only Mon–Sat; Sunday adds lunch).
- Wine-curious diners (the wine list is explicitly marketed).
