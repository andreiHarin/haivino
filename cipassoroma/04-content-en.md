## Translation observations (for our Haivino EN content)

- The IT→EN translation is **mostly literal** and would benefit from a native-EN pass (some awkward phrasings: *"At Google, we boast…"*, *"I always"*, *"4th venue in Italy to have left a positive impact"*).
- **Numbers are localized only partially** ("4,8" stays IT in some places, "4.8" in others).
- **Hours notation** stays day-name in English (Monday/Saturday) — fine.
- **Tagline rhyme is sacrificed** in EN ("per il posto / ci torno per il gusto" doesn't rhyme in EN). Worth crafting our own bilingual taglines rather than literal translations.
